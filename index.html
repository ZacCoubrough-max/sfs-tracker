Future Considerations
Planned features and enhancements for the SEA Fulfillment Hub

✦ AI-Powered Summaries

Generate plain-English performance summaries from your live data. Weekly recap, action items, movers & trends, or stakeholder email format. One click, ready to share.

📈 Year-on-Year Comparison

Compare the same week or quarter across years. W07 2026 vs W07 2025 side by side, per store. Data structure already supports it.

🔍 Store Benchmarking

Cross-country benchmarking to compare equivalent stores in MY, SG and TH. Identify which market sets the standard.

🔔 Automated Alerts Digest

Weekly email or Teams digest sent automatically after upload. Highlights Tier D stores, missed revenue, biggest movers.

🎯 Store Recovery Plans

Set individual store targets beyond network-wide targets. Track recovery plans for Tier D stores with target score and deadline.

📋 Programme Expansion

Add Same Day Delivery or Returns performance as additional programme tabs using the same country and scoring architecture.
